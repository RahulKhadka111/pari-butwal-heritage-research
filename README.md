# 🏛️ Pari Butwal Heritage Research

A historical and socio-cultural study of Pari Butwal, the origin of modern Butwal City[cite: 1]. Through extensive fieldwork, interviews with elderly residents, and cross-referencing historical texts, this project documents critical heritage sites and the shifting socio-economic landscape of the region[cite: 1].

## 📋 Research Scope & Key Focus Areas
*   **Jitgadhi Fort:** Documenting its historical significance, the strategic victory of the Gorkhali Sena, and current preservation needs[cite: 1].
*   **Ancient Architecture:** Mapping the structural integrity and passive thermal design of the 350+ year-old traditional houses of old Batauli[cite: 1].
*   **Interfaith Coexistence:** Exploring the deep cultural and religious harmony built over generations between Hindu and Muslim communities[cite: 1].
*   **The Developmental Gap:** Analyzing the historical and modern contrast and infrastructural disparities between Batauli and Khasauli[cite: 1].
