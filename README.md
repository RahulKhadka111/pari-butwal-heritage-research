# 🏛️ Pari Butwal Heritage Research

A historical and socio-cultural study of Pari Butwal, the origin of modern Butwal City. Through extensive fieldwork, interviews with elderly residents, and cross-referencing historical texts, this project documents critical heritage sites and the shifting socio-economic landscape of the region.

## 📋 Research Scope & Key Focus Areas
*   **Jitgadhi Fort:** Documenting its historical significance and current preservation status.
*   **Ancient Architecture:** Mapping the traditional houses of old Batauli.
*   **Interfaith Coexistence:** Exploring the cultural and religious harmony built over generations.
*   **The Developmental Gap:** Analyzing the historical and modern contrast between Batauli and Khasauli.
